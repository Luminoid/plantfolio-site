Features

Plants & Collections

  • Add plants manually (name, light, humidity, drainage, soil) or choose from 800+ suggestions with care info, toxicity, and USDA zones.
  • Organize into collectionse.g. living room, balcony, or plants in the same pot.

Watering & Calendar

  • See what's overdue, due today, or coming up, or view the full month calendar.
  • Watering adapts to the season automatically; log past watering, mark in bulk, or water a whole collection in one tap.

Photos

  • Dates are pulled from your photos. Categorize by growth, flowers, problems, or pests.
  • Full-screen view; edit dates, crop, and bulk select or delete.

Home Screen Widgets

  • Small: up-next watering.
  • Medium: calendar month with counts.

Reminders

  • Daily watering reminders at your preferred time, only for plants you're actively caring for.

Care Logs

  • Track growth, fertilization, and pruning with full history for each; add research notes per plant.

Your Data

  • Data stays on your device or iCloud; we don't store it on our servers. Export and import full backups; iCloud sync across devices.

Language & Settings

  • English, Spanish, and Chinese; customize date format and temperature units.
