Features

Plants & Collections

  • Add plants manually with names, light, humidity, drainage, soilor pick from 800+ suggestions with care info, toxicity, and USDA zones
  • Organize into collections like kitchen or balcony. Search, sort, filter, or archive as needed.

Watering & Calendar

  • See what's overdue, due today, or coming upor view the full month calendar
  • Watering adapts to the season automatically. Log past watering, mark in bulk, or water a whole collection with one tap.

Photos

Home Screen Widgets
