Plantfolio Plus

Plant Care, Growth & Discovery

Your plants, one place. Identify plants three ways—AI recognition, 952+ offline suggestions, or on-device ML for 7,800+ species. Seasonal watering calendar that adjusts automatically, detailed care logs with growth trends, and unlimited photos across 7 categories. Discover wild plants with the sightings journal and interactive map, share beautiful plant cards, and sync everything across devices with iCloud.

App Preview

Features

🌿

Plants & Collections

  • Three ways to identify: AI-powered recognition with care auto-fill (choose from 6 Gemini models or enter a custom model ID), 952+ offline plant suggestions, or on-device ML covering 7,800+ species.
  • Track name, light, humidity, drainage, soil, pot material, native origin, growth rate, propagation methods, and seasonal watering—all in one profile. AI notes render with markdown formatting.
  • Organize into collections—living room, balcony, same pot—and manage or water them together.
📅

Watering & Calendar

  • Set per-season watering intervals that adjust automatically as seasons change—spring, summer, fall, winter.
  • Log watering with intensity (misting, light, normal, soaked, bottom watering) and notes. Logs preserve exact time of day.
  • List view shows overdue, today, and upcoming; month view gives a visual overview with daily counts.
  • Reschedule, mark in bulk, or water a whole collection in one tap.
📸

Care Journal & Photos

  • Unified care history: watering, growth, fertilization, pruning, and status checks in one view. Filter by type with quick-tap chips.
  • AI-powered plant status checks—snap a photo for a health assessment with follow-up questions, conversation history, and a detailed report saved to the journal.
  • Unlimited photos per plant across 7 categories. Dates extracted from EXIF automatically.
🗺️

Sightings & Map

  • Log wild plants with Quick Scan—AI identifies species on the spot. Batch add up to 10 sightings at once with auto GPS from photos or device.
  • Interactive map with photo annotations, cluster badges, and tap-to-preview.
📱

Home Screen Widgets

  • Up Next—see which plants need watering at a glance.
  • Calendar—month view with daily watering counts.
  • Scan & Identify—open the camera for instant plant ID.
🔔

Reminders

  • Daily watering reminders at your preferred time, only for plants you're actively caring for. Background refresh keeps notification content and badge count up to date.
🔗

Sharing

  • Generate beautiful plant cards—single or curated lists—as shareable images for social media or messaging.
  • Share photos from other apps directly into Plantfolio with the Share Extension—add plants or sightings without switching apps.
  • Export your watering schedule as an ICS calendar file compatible with any calendar app.
🔒

Your Data

  • Everything stays on your device or iCloud—we never see it. Full ZIP backup with export and import; merge or replace on restore.
  • Find plants instantly with Spotlight Search—swipe down on your home screen and type a name. All indexing stays on-device.
🌐

Language & Settings

  • English, Spanish, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese. Display plant names in a second language. Customize date format, temperature units, pot and soil materials, and more.

Why Choose Plantfolio Plus

Works without internet

On-device ML identifies 7,800+ species offline. 952+ plant suggestions available without a connection. No account required.

Adapts to your seasons

Watering intervals adjust automatically as seasons change—set it once, and the calendar follows your climate year-round.

Beyond the garden

Take your plant hobby outdoors. Quick Scan identifies wild plants on the spot and pins them to an interactive map.

One app, every device

iPhone, iPad, and Mac—with full keyboard and mouse support on iPad and Mac, including ⌘1–⌘4 to switch tabs, ⌘N for a new plant, and ⌘F for search. iCloud keeps everything in sync.

Own your data

No servers, no accounts. Everything stays on your device or iCloud. Export a full ZIP backup anytime and restore with merge or replace.
