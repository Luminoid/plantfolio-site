Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Plantfolio Care ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Data Controller

The data controller for your personal information is:

  • Name: Chunhou Li
  • Email: diveintothefog@gmail.com

If you require a physical address for legal purposes, please contact us using the email address provided above.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Plantfolio Care, you may provide the following types of information:

  • Plant Data: Information about your plants including names, scientific names, plant types, descriptions, care notes, research notes, and care schedules
  • Photos: Images of your plants that you choose to add to the App
  • Care Records: Watering logs, fertilization records, pruning activities, growth measurements, and related notes
  • Collections: Information about how you organize your plants into collections
  • Settings: Your preferences for date formats, language settings, notification preferences, and sorting options

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Photo Metadata: The App may access EXIF data from photos you add, including dates when photos were taken, to automatically populate date fields
  • Device Information: Basic device information necessary for app functionality (iOS version, device model) may be collected by Apple's App Store and analytics services

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address) unless you contact us directly
  • Location data
  • Payment information (the App does not process payments)
  • Browsing history or activity outside the App

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • To enable features such as watering reminders, calendar views, and plant organization
  • To store and sync your data across your devices (when using iCloud)
  • To improve the App's functionality and user experience
  • To respond to your support requests or inquiries

2.1 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, we process your personal data based on the following legal bases:

  • Contract Performance: Processing is necessary to provide the App's core functionality and features that you have requested
  • Legitimate Interest: Processing for app improvements and user experience enhancements
  • Consent: When you explicitly choose to enable optional features such as iCloud sync or notifications

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Your plant data, photos, and settings are stored locally on your device using Core Data. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3.2 iCloud Sync

If you choose to enable iCloud sync (when available), your data will be stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is:

  • Encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple's iCloud service
  • Subject to Apple's iCloud Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy
  • Accessible only to you through devices signed into your iCloud account
  • Not accessible to us or any third parties

3.3 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Data is stored using iOS's secure storage mechanisms
  • We do not have access to your data stored locally or in iCloud
  • No data is transmitted to external servers under our control

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Apple Services

The App uses the following Apple services:

  • App Store: For app distribution and updates. Apple may collect certain information as described in their Privacy Policy.
  • iCloud: For optional data synchronization. Data stored in iCloud is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Push Notifications: For watering reminders. Notification data is processed by Apple's notification service.

We encourage you to review Apple's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your information: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

4.2 Wikipedia Integration

The App may provide links to Wikipedia pages for plant information. When you access Wikipedia, you are subject to Wikipedia's Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data except in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly choose to share data (e.g., through iCloud sync)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid legal requests
  • Service Providers: We may use Apple's services (iCloud, App Store) which process data according to their own privacy policies

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data directly within the App
  • Modification: You can edit, update, or delete your plant data, photos, and records at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete individual plants, photos, or records, or delete the App to remove all local data
  • Export: Your data is stored locally and can be accessed through your device

6.2 Notification Preferences

You can enable or disable notifications at any time through the App's settings. You can also manage notification permissions through your iOS device settings.

6.3 iCloud Data

If you use iCloud sync, you can manage your iCloud data through your Apple ID settings. You can disable iCloud sync at any time through the App or your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Plantfolio Care is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children:

  • United States (COPPA): We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.
  • European Economic Area (GDPR): We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16 (or the age of digital consent in your country, which may be 13-16). If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

8. International Users and Data Transfers

If you are using the App from outside the United States, please note that:

  • Your data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries where Apple operates
  • For users in the European Economic Area, data transfers to countries outside the EEA are subject to appropriate safeguards, including:
    • Apple's compliance with Standard Contractual Clauses and other data transfer mechanisms approved by the European Commission
    • Apple's participation in the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework (where applicable)
  • By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these locations in accordance with the safeguards described above
  • We comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR for users in the European Economic Area

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.

10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing of your data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below. We will respond to your request within one month (or inform you if we need additional time).

Right to Lodge a Complaint: If you believe we have not addressed your concerns adequately, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority. For a list of supervisory authorities, please visit: https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en

11. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the App. When you delete data within the App or uninstall the App, the data is removed from your device. If you use iCloud sync, data may remain in your iCloud account until you delete it through your Apple ID settings.

11.1 Automated Decision-Making and Profiling

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects or significantly affects you. The App's features, such as watering schedule calculations, are based on the information you provide and do not constitute automated decision-making under GDPR.

11.2 Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that may affect your personal information, we will:

  • Notify relevant supervisory authorities within 72 hours (as required by GDPR for EEA users)
  • Notify affected users without undue delay if the breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and freedoms
  • Provide clear information about the nature of the breach and steps being taken to address it

Given that we do not store your data on our servers and all data remains on your device or in your iCloud account, the risk of a data breach affecting your information is minimal.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing notice through the App when significant changes are made

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
